I started birding when I was in my final years of undergraduate studies. I used to climb on top of our [H3 hostel](https://gymkhana.iitb.ac.in/~hostel3/) terrace at our [university](https://www.iitb.ac.in/) and observe them every now and then. After a couple of weeks I was still very fascinated by this and started photographing and creating a catalogue of these beautiful specimens. Here are some of them which I photographed between 2024-25. After I graduated I couldn't find time to do these activities, I hope in a couple of years when things settle down I will again comeback to it
